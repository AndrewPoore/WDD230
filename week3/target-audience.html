Description of target audience:

The target audience will be new and experienced business owners alike.

Two personas:

Mike

  • Age: 32
  • Employment: Property Owner
  • Education: College Education
  • Marital Status: Single
  • Reason for visiting site: Looking for landscapers for properties.

Tyler

  • Age: 44
  • Employment: Dentist
  • Education: College Education
  • Marital Status: Married
  • Reason for visiting site: Looking for web developers to build his office's website.

Five scenarios:

  1. Companies looking for interns posting job opportunities.
  2. Health food store looking for local sourced produce from an agricultural company.
  3. A resturaunt manager looking for suppliers of special kitchen appliances.
  4. A local business owner looking for accountants to track cash flow.
  5. Companies looking for HR training
