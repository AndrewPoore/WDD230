Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to bring together entrepreneurs, so that they can network with each other and help grow their businesses. For example, two businesses can market their services on the site, and if it coincides with a need that the other business has, they can collaborate how they see fit.
