Typography Usage:

"Red Hat Mono" will be used on all headings and larger text, while Arial will be used for larger masses of text for legibility purposes. Red Hat Mono is an elegant and clean serif font with enough pop to create extra interest when needed. Arial is a classic for a reason and will create great accessibility for users.
