Target Audience:

Anyone who wants to know what the weather is. The age range could vary drastically. I am expecting ages from 12-70. Income, education, family status, and many other variables can also vary drastically. The site will most definitely be accessed mostly on mobile devices. Other than a vast, general audience, I can imagine one other demographic. Different industries of business are reliant on the weather. The first that comes to mind is construction. Scientific research, ski resorts, and the postal service are some other industries that are dependant on weather circumstances.

Personas:

Jason

  • Occupation: Firefighter
  • Demographics and Education: 45 year old male with a high school education.
  • Motivation for using the site: He likes to check weather conditions to make sure he's ready to go out on a call.

Nancy

  • Occupation: Postal worker
  • Demographics and Education: 40 year old female with some college experience.
  • Motivation for using the site: Nancy likes to check the weather before she goes to work so she can dress accordingly.
